Workouts

A good workout routine helps you progress and avoid plateaus. Here's a weekly intermediate-level workout routine:

Monday – Chest and Triceps

  • Bench press – 4x10
  • Dips – 3x12
  • Incline press – 4x10

Tuesday – Back and Biceps

  • Pull-ups – 3x10
  • Barbell rows – 4x12
  • Bicep curls – 4x10

Wednesday – Legs

  • Squats – 4x12
  • Leg press – 3x15
  • Lunges – 3x12 per leg

Thursday – Shoulders and Abs

  • Military press – 4x10
  • Lateral raises – 3x12
  • Crunches – 3x25

Friday – Full Body or Cardio

  • Burpees – 3x15
  • Jump rope – 5 minutes
  • Quick circuit with minimal rest

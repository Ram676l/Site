Nutrition

Good nutrition is 70% of the results. It doesn't help to train hard if you don't eat well.

Key Foods:

  • Proteins: chicken, tuna, eggs, tofu, lean meat
  • Good Carbs: oats, brown rice, potatoes, fruits
  • Healthy Fats: avocado, nuts, olive oil

Tips:

• Eat 5 meals a day in moderate portions
• Drink at least 2 liters of water
• Don't skip breakfast
• Avoid fried foods, sugar, and sodas

Supplements (optional):

Protein powder, creatine, multivitamins, and BCAA, always under a specialist's recommendation.
